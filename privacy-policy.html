Privacy Policy

Zivon AI respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how Zivon AI collects, uses, and protects your information.

Information We Access

Zivon AI accesses YouTube Analytics and YouTube Data API information such as channel metrics, video performance, titles, and engagement statistics.

How We Use Information

Data is used solely to analyze performance and provide AI-powered recommendations inside YouTube Studio. Zivon AI does not sell, share, or use data for advertising purposes.

Data Storage

Access tokens are securely stored and used only for authenticated API requests. Data is retained only as long as required to provide features.

User Control

Users can revoke Zivon AI’s access at any time through their Google Account permissions.

Third-Party Sharing

Zivon AI does not share user data with third parties.
